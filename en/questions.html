Questions

What is an extrait de parfum at 25% concentration?

Extrait de parfum is perfumery's most concentrated form: our compositions contain 25% pure concentrate, where an eau de parfum typically holds 12 to 18%. The sillage is denser and more enveloping — a few sprays are enough.

How long does an extrait de parfum last?

Depending on skin and season, a 25% extrait usually lasts the whole day — eight to twelve hours on skin, longer on fabric. Oro Rojo, Karès Blan and Ahi Pō are composed to endure.

Are the editions truly limited?

Yes. The 10 ml format of each fragrance in the Paradise Islands collection is released as a numbered limited edition — 300 bottles per reference — and every bottle is engraved with its island. Your bottle's number is inscribed on its base.

Where are the fragrances composed?

All three extraits of the Paradise Islands collection are composed in Grasse, the cradle of haute parfumerie, by perfumer Guillaume Cresp.

Is a sample included with my order?

Yes — with every 10 ml travel case, the 2 ml discovery samples of the other two references are included, so you can explore the whole collection.

How is my order shipped?

Every order ships with tracked delivery, in a double-wall kraft carton with a sealed tear-proof security pouch. In France and nearby Europe: four Chronopost modes, home delivery against signature or Shop2Shop pick-up point (collection with a secure PIN code or QR code). Across the rest of Europe: Chronopost home delivery against signature. International: FedEx, duties & taxes included (DDP). Shipping fees are calculated at checkout. You can follow your parcel at any time on our Track my order page, using your order number and email.

Can I return a fragrance?

You have 14 days to return an item by mail in its original condition. Return shipping remains at your charge. Write to contact@absignatureparis.com and we will guide you.

Where can I read reviews of AB Signature Paris fragrances?

All three fragrances are listed on Fragrantica, and our client ratings are published on Trustpilot. The first verified reviews will appear on the fragrance pages as soon as the first orders are delivered.
