Paradise Islands · Three extraits de parfum

There are journeys that no map will ever chart.

Three islands, three hours of the world : the sunset of Aruba, the high noon of La Digue, the sacred night of Moorea. AB Signature Paris has engraved them in glass, sealed them with a golden cap, signed them with a single line.

Follow the line.

Chapter I — Aruba · The sunset hour

Oro Rojo

Incandescent fruity-woody · Extrait de parfum 25 %

Oro Rojo is an incandescent fruity-woody extrait. A voluptuous cherry, heightened by a blazing saffron, coils around a silky orange blossom and a powdery iris, before plunging into a deep base of dark cacao and oud — a sensory journey through Aruba, sweet, spiced and mysterious.

The incandescence of Aruba’s sunset, where cherry embraces oud.

Chapter II — La Digue · High noon

Karès Blan

Solar musky floral · Extrait de parfum 25 %

Karès Blan is a radiant, solar musky floral extrait. A sparkling bergamot, lifted by powdery almond and luminous aldehydes, coils around a soft, cottony white heart of orange blossom, white flowers and lily of the valley, before settling into a creamy base of musk and vanilla — a sensory journey through the Seychelles, elegant, luminous and caressing.

The white caress of the Seychelles, orange blossom and vanilla.

Bergamot, Almond, Aldehydes

Heart

Orange blossom, White flowers, Lily of the valley

Chapter III — Moorea · The sacred night

Ahi Pō

Mystical woody leather · Extrait de parfum 25 %

An olfactory journey into the heart of Polynesia : Ahi Pō captures the mystery of a sacred night on the waters of the Pacific. Beneath the starlit sky, tanned bark and leather intertwine with the deep black of dew-pearled blackcurrant. A contemplative fragrance signed AB Signature Paris, revealing the woody, leathered soul of the paradise islands.

Sacred island night, where noble leather entwines with white flowers.

Top

Blackcurrant, Cardamom

Heart

Leather, White flowers, Rose
